About Stawks

What is this?

Stawks (Stock Quick Gains) is a web app that lets you explore stock and crypto data, then train machine learning models to predict future prices. It started as a masters project and has been modernized with a full dashboard experience.

This is a proof of concept -- not financial advice. Don't YOLO your savings based on what an LSTM tells you.

Models Available
LSTM Neural Network

A recurrent neural network that learns temporal patterns in price data. Trained on historical OHLCV data with a sliding window approach.

Random Forest Classifier

An ensemble method using 500 decision trees to classify whether a stock will go up or down the next day based on engineered features.
